About Us

Sunnies Face came out of our team's obsession with makeup

We always had the “I like this, but…” dilemma when buying beauty products. Luxury brands were too expensive while more affordable brands did not have the best-in-class formulas and elegant packaging we were looking for. So, after years of not finding the perfect in-between, we created Sunnies Face.

Locally inspired, globally desired

Born out of the Philippines, Sunnies Face was formulated with Asia's rich array of skin tones in mind. While locally inspired, our luxurious formulas and thoughtfully created color edits have made Sunnies Face beloved by a global audience

Feel good about looking good

We believe in beauty that is good for you. Sunnies Face has always been and will continue to be consciously made and toxic-free. Our products are proudly paraben-free, cruelty-free, and fragrance-free..
